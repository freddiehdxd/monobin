About

Monobin is a tiny meta-framework with one opinion: the best deploy is a single binary you can scp and run.

  • HTML-first — SSR + SSG by default, JS is opt-in.
  • Islands — interactive components hydrate in isolation.
  • Zero-ops — no node_modules in production, no PM2 for the app.

This page ships zero JavaScript.
